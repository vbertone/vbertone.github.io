Runge-Kutta (RK) ODE solvers.

These functions solve the ordinary differential equation (ODE):

dy / dt = f(t,y)

where:

dy = rk4(f(t,y))

so differentiation between lower and upper:

y += dy(t,y,dt)

U is the type of the 'y' object.
