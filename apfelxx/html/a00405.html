Detailed Description

O(as) gluon coefficient function for F2.

Massless limit of the massive Netral Current coefficient functions. In the following "xi" indicates the ratio Q^2 / mh^2 and "xiF" is equal to mh^2 / muF^2. See Appendix D of https://arxiv.org/pdf/hep-ph/9601302.pdf. The suffix "_c" in the classes below stands for "constant", that is no dependence on "xi" or "xiF". "_l" or "_l2" instead means that that term is proportional to log(xi) or log^2(xi), respectively. While "_f" means proportional to log(xiF) amd "_lf" proportional to log(xi)

  • log(xiF).
