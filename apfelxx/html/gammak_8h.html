Cusp anomalous dimension. Coefficients of the

γK anomalous dimension. The expressions up to O(αs3) are taken from eq. (59) https://arxiv.org/pdf/1705.07167.pdf. While the expressions at are taken from O(αs4) from eqs. (4.1) and (4.2) of https://arxiv.org/pdf/1805.09638.pdf. A better approximation to the O(αs4) is found in https://arxiv.org/pdf/1912.12920v2.pdf.

Note
All the expressions do not include an overall factor CF or CA.
