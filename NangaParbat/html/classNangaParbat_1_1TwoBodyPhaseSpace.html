Detailed Description

Class for the calculation of the phase-space reduction factor due to cuts on the single outgoing lepton in Drell-Yan production. The relevant process is:
γ(q) → l+(k1) + l-(k2)
with:
kT,1(2) > pT,min
|η1(2)| < ηmax

Examples
IntegrationTest.cc.
